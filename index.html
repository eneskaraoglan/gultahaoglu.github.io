About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Engineering, Karadeniz Technical University (KTU). My research lies at the intersection of Cyber Security, Artificial Intelligence, and Multimedia Forensics.

I am passionate about developing robust AI solutions for digital media integrity, specifically focusing on detecting manipulated content such as deepfakes and analyzing audio/visual anomalies for forensic investigations.
