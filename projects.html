Research Focus & Projects

ÇAĞRI MERKEZLERİ İÇİN DERİN SAHTE SES TESPİT MODÜLÜ

This ongoing TÜBİTAK 1505 University-Industry Collaboration project focuses on developing a robust, real-time deepfake audio detection module specifically tailored for call center environments. The system aims to identify synthetic and manipulated voice patterns to prevent fraud and enhance institutional security using advanced AI and signal processing methodologies.

TÜBİTAK 1505 2025 - 2027 Audio Forensics

Sayısal Seslerdeki Sahteciliğin Tespiti ve Sahte Ses Kesitlerinin Lokalizasyonu

Supported by the TÜBİTAK 1001 program, this project investigates the detection of tampering in digital audio files. Our research establishes advanced deep learning architectures to not only classify audio as genuine or forged but also to accurately pinpoint the specific segments where splicing or copy-move forgery has occurred.

[[Project Details on AVESIS]]
TÜBİTAK 1001 2022 - 2024 Deep Learning

Detection of Forgery in Digital Audios and Localization of Fraudulent Sounds

A comprehensive TÜBİTAK 1001 research initiative aimed at tackling the increasing threat of auditory manipulation. By utilizing super-resolution spectrograms and novel neural network models, the project significantly improved the robustness of digital audio forgery detection against various post-processing attacks.
