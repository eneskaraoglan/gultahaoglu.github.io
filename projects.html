Research Focus & Projects

Multimedia Forensics

We are working on ensuring the integrity and authenticity of digital media (images, audio, video). Our algorithms detect manipulations, copy-move attacks, and splicing, aiming to restore trust in digital content.

Image Integrity Forgery Detection

AI-Powered Deepfake Detection

This project focuses on developing cutting-edge deep learning models (such as ResNeXt and CNNs) to automatically identify synthetic media generated by Generative AI. We analyze both visual artifacts and auditory spectral anomalies.

Deep Learning Generative AI Audio/Video Analysis

Intelligent Signal Processing

Applying AI and machine learning algorithms to process and analyze complex signal data for feature extraction and pattern recognition. This includes super-resolution spectrogram analysis for enhanced audio forensics.

Signal Processing Feature Extraction
