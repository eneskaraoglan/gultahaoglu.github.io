Research Focus & Projects

Multimedia Forensics

Ensuring the integrity and authenticity of digital media (images, audio, video) through advanced analysis techniques to detect manipulations and forgeries. This includes detecting copy-move attacks, splicing, and other forms of digital tampering.

AI-Powered Deepfake Detection

Developing cutting-edge deep learning models (such as ResNeXt and CNNs) to automatically identify synthetic media generated by generative AI. We focus on detecting both visual artifacts and auditory anomalies in fake content.

Intelligent Signal Processing

Applying AI and machine learning algorithms to process and analyze complex signal data for feature extraction and pattern recognition. This research extends to super-resolution spectrograms for better audio analysis.
